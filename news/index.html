September 17, 2022

Dinixa ASi announcement

Dinixa ASi stands for Dinixa App Services instant. And it will be used for applications that need data as fast as possible like multiplayer games or live chat and drawing boards. It will not be 24/7 service like AS is so do not rely on in app chat or 1st party servers in games because we still do not have a real hosting for it.
