          Story of www.renatogr.cf Video

Before this service there was another video sharing platform made by me called RedLive. I gave up on RedLive because of stupid name and I wanted to start over and something that will fit in with other services so I came up with www.renatogr.cf Video. It had a clipbucket webapp and that did not work, almost at all. Only I was able to publish videos, register system did not work so I got mad and left the project. I tried few more webapps but non of them actually worked and project was left to die. Until www.renatogr.cf Home switched to custom written html then I also wrote html for www.renatogr.cf Video. It was really ugly and it did not support registering or any interaction than navigating and playing videos. Videos were uploaded by uploading it to root of the website and making html file for it and adding a hyper link to main file. And it was pretty bad but video playback was good what made it survive this long. This service was used once by me in a lesson where I showed how to do something and I posted that video to the service and added link to video in the lesson. And now, service got at least nicer looking discontinued page than it was. So yeah, that is that short and boring story of www.renatogr.cf Video service
