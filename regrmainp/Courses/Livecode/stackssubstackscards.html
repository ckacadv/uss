Stacks, substacks and cards



Creating and navigating throughout cards

To create new card go to your stack and press Ctrl+N to create new card. To navigate throughout cards is very simple

To go to first card press Ctrl+1

To go to previous card press Ctrl+2

To go to next card press Ctrl+3

To go to last card on your stack press Ctrl+4

To go to recent card press Ctrl+5

NOTE: This type of navigation can only be used while building an app in livecode!
