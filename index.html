rgDesktop 4.6

Yes, 4.6 is here and it brings some new, really needed features! There was minimal design changes but at least there were any. Tabs that got most attention were Apps and Account. Apps tab can now show in app screenshots and even download inside the app! Account page shows notification and the whole system is more secure now.
